Steps to Follow



  • Five objects appear side by side on the screen for 60 seconds. 
  • Memorize them and pay attention to their positions. 
  • After that time, answer to the questions about the position of the objects by selecting “Yes” or “No”. 
  • Click on the “ok” button when you have answered to all questions. 
  • Time is kept as the exercise appears. 
  • Time count ends as you press the “ok” button.
