Restrictions about perfect use of exercise 2.

For getting perfect result from exercise 2 please be careful about these issues. 
  • Click on the number or word not on the point  . As if it written  .1   and .2  and you want to join them with line you must click on 1 and 2 instead of . as they are just for connection.
  • Your system resolution must be 1280X1024 with operating system Windows .It is better if you have windows XP.
  • After finishing all connection user must click OK button for knowing result.

Steps to Follow



      
  • Some dots on the screen are associated with numbers or letters. Link them in ascending order, by clicking once on the number or letter. 
  • The system automatically draws a line between two consequently selected numbers or letters. 
  • If you find both numbers and letters in the same screen, alternate a number and a letter always in ascending order: the first number must be linked with the first letter of alphabet; the first letter with the second number; the second number with the second letter and so on until the end. 
  • To undo the last selection, click on the “undo” button. 
  • Only at the end of the path, click on the “ok” button.
  •  If the exercise is correctly performed a picture will be finally visible. 
  • Time is kept as the exercise appears. 
  • Time count ends as you press the “ok” button.
